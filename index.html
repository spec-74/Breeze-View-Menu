The Breeze View Story

Born from a passion for exceptional dining and breathtaking views, Breeze View Restaurant has been the crown jewel of Sh.Narudhoo since our inception. We believe that dining is not just about food, but about creating memorable experiences.

Our philosophy combines traditional Maldivian culinary heritage with modern techniques, using only the freshest locally-sourced ingredients. Each dish tells a story of our islands' rich culture and abundant natural resources.

Meet Our Head Chef

With over 20 years of international culinary experience, Chef Ahmed brings innovation and passion to every plate. His expertise in blending traditional flavors with contemporary presentation has earned us numerous accolades.
